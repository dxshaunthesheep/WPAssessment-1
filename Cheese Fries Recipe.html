Recipe for Cheese Fries:

These amazing cheese fries melt in your tongue and taste absolutely mesmerizing. The cheese is rich and creamy, while the fries crispy with just the right amount of salt.

Ingredients for the fries:

    Homemade Chips/Fries

  •  2-3 Large Potato About 400g ( 14oz ), Russet, Maris Piper, Desiree, King Edward 
  • ¼ tsp Black pepper or pepper to taste 
  • ¼ tsp Salt or salt to taste 
  • 3 tbsp Corn starch or potato starch 
  • Oil for frying Vegetable, sunflower, canola, or neutral flavour oil

    • Cheese Sauce

  • 250 ml Milk Whole milk or skimmed milk 
  • 150 g Mature Chedder About 5oz, grated 
  • 1 tbsp Butter Unsalted butter 
  • 1 tbsp Plain flour All-purpose flour or corn flour 
  • ¼ tsp Garlic granules or garlic powder ( Optional )  

Method to cook Cheese Fries

  1. For the homemade chips/fries,  peel the potatoes and chop them in fine french-fry pieces.
  2.  Then add some cold wate, and rinse the potato pieces.
  3.  Add water to a pan and bring it to a boil.
  4.  Then add the potats to the pan and boil until it's moderately soft. 
  5.  Then dry the potatos.
  6.  Add corn starch to the potatos and mix well.
  7.  Then deep fry the potatoes until golden brown.  
  8.  For the cheese sauce, melt butter into a pan.
  9.  Once the butter is fully melted, add flour to the mix. 
  10.  Mix it while also adding milk little by little to the mix until ithas a syrup-like texture.
  11.  Add cheddar cheese and mix well, until it becomes the required texture needed for cheese sauce. 
  12.  After that, just mix in the fries and the cheese untilyou have the perfect snack ever created.
