Characters

1. Walter White (Bryan Cranston)

  • Alias: Heisenberg
  • Role: A high school chemistry teacher turned meth kingpin.
  • Arc: Begins as a sympathetic, desperate man but becomes a ruthless, power-hungry criminal. His intelligence and pride drive his rise—and eventual downfall.
2. Jesse Pinkman (Aaron Paul)
  • Alias: Cap’n Cook
  • Role: Walter’s former student and partner in the meth business.
  • Arc: Starts as a small-time drug dealer but evolves into a deeply conflicted, guilt-ridden figure. His moral struggles contrast with Walter’s descent.

3. Skyler White (Anna Gunn)

  • Role: Walter’s wife, initially unaware of his crimes.
  • Arc: Begins as a supportive spouse but becomes entangled in Walter’s lies, leading to fear, complicity, and defiance.

4. Hank Schrader (Dean Norris)

  • Role: DEA agent and Walter’s brother-in-law.
  • Arc: Starts as a brash, confident cop but faces psychological and physical trauma as he unknowingly hunts Heisenberg.

5. Gustavo "Gus" Fring (Giancarlo Esposito)

  • Role: Calm but terrifying drug lord and fast-food chain owner (Los Pollos Hermanos).
  • Arc: A methodical, calculating businessman who sees Walter as both an asset and a threat.

6. Saul Goodman (Bob Odenkirk)

  • Role: Slippery, morally flexible criminal lawyer.
  • Arc: Provides legal (and illegal) advice to Walter and Jesse, often with comic relief. Later gets his own spin-off (Better Call Saul). Key Traits: Witty, opportunistic, survivalist.

7. Mike Ehrmantraut (Jonathan Banks)

  • Role: Gus’s fixer/enforcer, later works with Walter.
  • Arc: A no-nonsense ex-cop with a strict moral code (despite being a criminal). His loyalty and professionalism clash with Walter’s recklessness.

8. Marie Schrader (Betsy Brandt)

  • Role: Skyler’s sister and Hank’s wife.
  • Arc: Initially a quirky, somewhat nosy character, she becomes a key emotional anchor as Hank’s investigation unfolds. Key Traits: Supportive, anxious, occasionally petty.

9. Tuco Salamanca (Raymond Cruz)

  • Role: Unhinged cartel enforcer.
  • Arc: A volatile, unpredictable antagonist in early seasons. Key Traits: Psychotic, aggressive, explosive.

10. The Cousins (Leonel & Marco Salamanca)

  • Role: Silent, Terminator-like cartel assassins.
  • Arc: Seek revenge on those who wronged their family.

11. Todd Alquist (Jesse Plemons)

  • Role: A sociopathic enforcer working with Walter in Season 5.
  • Arc: Represents the banality of evil—polite but utterly remorseless.

12. Walter White Jr. / Flynn (RJ Mitte)

  • Role: Walter and Skyler’s son, who has cerebral palsy.
  • Arc: Struggles with his father’s secrets and the family’s collapse. Key Traits: Idealistic, frustrated, loyal.
